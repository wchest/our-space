Our Values

Built on what actually matters.

We're not a see-and-be-seen scene. We're a space where people show up for each other.

Community-First

We show up for each other. This isn't networking — it's genuine mutual support, accountability, and care.

Centering Marginalized Voices

Queer and BIPOC members' needs come first. We build with, not just for, the most impacted people in our community.

Accessible by Design

Sliding scale pricing ensures your financial situation doesn't determine your access to community and workspace.

Scrappy & Real

Good enough over perfect. Authenticity over polish. Come as you are — we're building this together.

Who We're Looking For

Is this your space?

You might be a great fit if…

  • ✓ You work independently and want genuine community
  • ✓ You're building something meaningful — a business, practice, creative work, or organizing effort
  • ✓ You want to know your coworkers, not just work near them
  • ✓ You value diversity, equity, and belonging
  • ✓ You're willing to contribute to community, not just consume

Probably not the right fit if…

  • ✗ You just need a desk and prefer to be left alone
  • ✗ You're on calls all day
  • ✗ You're looking for fancy amenities or corporate polish
  • ✗ You're not comfortable with intentional community norms
  • ✗ You can't commit to showing up — literally and figuratively
